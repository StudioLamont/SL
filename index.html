Studio Lamont


¶ Studio Lamont is a book design and typography studio specialising in printed books, with a focus on fine lettering and elegant layout. The studio’s work is rooted in a hands-on knowledge of bookcraft, from thread to flyleaf. Whether it’s a limited edition or a mass-market paperback, we take an artisan’s approach to everything we do.
¶ The studio’s clients include publishers, universities, museums, libraries, and authors. If you need a beautifully crafted book, get in touch: hello@studiolamont.co.uk
¶ The studio is headed by Luke Allan, who received his mfa in Book Arts from the Center for the Book, University of Iowa, where he taught book design and trained as a calligrapher, bookbinder, paper-maker, and letterpress printer. He is currently a doctoral fellow at the University of Cambridge, researching poetry and typography.
